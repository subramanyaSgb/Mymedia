v1.9.0 - Songs/Books/Games get their own vocabulary; albums; OTT badges

- Per-category status vocabulary: Songs "Want to Listen/Saved", Books
  "Want to Read/Read", Games "Want to Play/Played" (movies still Watch).
  Songs collapse the middle "in progress" state. Wired through detail
  segmented control, StatusPicker, list chips + bulk actions, stats.
- Songs no longer mixed with movies: Home splits "Recently added" (video)
  from "Recently saved" (songs/books/games); Continue Watching is video-only.
  Library splits "Watch" from "Listen / Read / Play".
- Album screen: tap a song -> full soundtrack/album with per-track save and
  "Save whole album"; song search rows link to their album; search a movie
  name -> its soundtrack. Album id stored on saved songs.
- OTT provider badge: saved movies/series show their India streaming
  provider logo on the card (library grids + home rail).
- Stats are category-aware (Saved/Read/Played, Hours hidden for songs/books).
